Exploring GTNP
App Information

The map displays Grand Teton National Park Points of Interest, Services, Trails, and the Park Boundary. Zoom in and pan around the map through scrolling or pinching fingers and hold to drag the map. Click on any feature in the map to see more information. The trailheads provide extensive information regarding the trails accessed by that trailhead. To search for a location, use the search box. To get your current location, use the button below the search box. In the menu, go to 'Directions' to get directions between two locations. Go to 'Filter Features' to filter the Points of Interest, Services, and Trails in the map or to turn on/off map layers, including turning on the user top-ranked Points of Interest layer (contains stars at all of the Points of Interest that users have voted on for their favorite with a comment in the popup of information). Go to 'Submit Rankings' to vote for your favorite Point of Interest with a comment. Once you submit your vote, a star in the User Rankings layer will be added to the map with your comment in the popup of information. Go to 'Ranking Table' to see a list of all Points of Interest that have been voted on for a favorite with the count of votes. For any menu form that opens, the map will be below (scroll down to see the map).
