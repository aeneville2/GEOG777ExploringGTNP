Exploring GTNP
App Information

Welcome to the Exploring Grand Teton National Park Application! The map displays Grand Teton National Park Points of Interest, Services, Trails, and the Park Boundary. Zoom in and pan around the map directly through interacting with the map either by touchscreen or with a pointing device. Click on any feature in the map to see more information. The trailheads provide extensive information regarding the trails accessed by that trailhead. To search for a location in the map, use the search box. To get your current location in the map, click the button below the search box. Click the button again to remove your location from the map. To get directions between two locations, click the 'Toggle Directions' button in the map and either type in two places into the search bars that pop open or click two places in the map. To turn off the directions, hit the 'Toggle Directions' button again.

In the menu: Go to 'App Information' to see this information screen again. Go to 'Map Legend' to view the legend for how the features are styled in the map. Go to 'Filter Features' to filter the Points of Interest, Services, and Trails layers in the map or to turn on/off map layers, including turning on the user top-ranked Points of Interest layer (contains stars at all of the Points of Interest that users have voted on for their favorite with numbers on the star representing how many stars are clustered there and user comments in the popup of information displayed after clicking individual stars). Go to 'Submit Rankings' to vote for your favorite Point of Interest with a comment. Once you submit your vote, your comment will be added to the popup of information for the star at that Point of Interest in the map. Go to 'Ranking Table' to see a list of all Points of Interest that have been voted on for a favorite with the count of votes. All of the menu forms open on top of the map.
