Exploring GTNP
App Information

The map displays POIs (Points of Interest), Services, Trails, and the Grand Teton Park Boundary. Click on any feature in the map to see more information. To search for a location, use the search box. To get your current location, use the button below the search box. In the menu, go to "Directions" to get directions between two locations. Go to "Filter Features" to filter the POIs, Services, and Trails in the map or turn on/off map layers, including turning on the user top-ranked POIs layer. Go to "Submit Rankings" to vote for your favorite POI with a comment that will then be added to the map. Got to "Ranking Table" to see a list of all POIs that have been voted on for a favorite with their vote (ranking) count.

Type in a starting and ending point or select starting and ending points in the map to receive directions.
Select your favorite POI and provide an appropriate comment about why it is your favorite to help other visitors.
